Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · Corelith Nexus

Your link data never leaves your browser. Extraction and export happen entirely on your device.

1. Who We Are

LinkHarvest Pro is a Chrome extension developed and maintained by Corelith Nexus. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account
  • Authentication tokens — stored locally in your browser via Chrome Storage to keep you signed in
  • License plan — Free or Pro status stored to enable or restrict features

We do not collect, store or transmit any link data, page content, browsing history, or personal information beyond what is listed above.

3. What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • The links or URLs you extract
  • The websites you visit
  • Your browsing history
  • Any financial, health, or sensitive personal data

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you when you sign in
  • Validate your license plan (Free or Pro)
  • Process payments via Stripe (for Pro subscribers)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and license data storage. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions. Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in with Google. Privacy Policy

6. Broken Link Checker

When you use the Broken Link Checker feature (Pro), the extension makes HTTP requests directly from your browser to the URLs found on the page in order to check their status codes. These requests originate from your browser and are not routed through our servers. We do not log or store the URLs checked.

7. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API. Your license information is stored in Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled, meaning only you can access your own data.

8. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights (GDPR & LGPD)

If you are located in the European Union or Brazil, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

LinkHarvest Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
