FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does it work on every website?+

Yes. LinkHarvest Pro works on any website — including dynamic pages rendered by JavaScript. If you can see links on screen, the extension can extract them.

Is my data safe? Does it leave my computer?+

Your link data never leaves your browser. Extraction and export happen entirely on your machine. We only communicate with our servers for authentication and license validation.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?+

Absolutely. Cancel from your account dashboard in one click. You keep Pro access until the end of the billing period. No questions asked.

How many links can I extract per day?+

No limits. Extract links from as many pages as you need, as often as you need.

Is there a free trial for Pro?+

The Free plan is permanent and includes CSV/JSON export. You can use all core functionality before deciding to upgrade to Pro for Excel, multi-tab and broken link checking.
